Search

Read

After you have searched for a book you can get a discription and can be directed to a place to purchase your new book. Once you get the book you can read it and add it to your Shelf.

Shelf

Once you find a book you like you can add it to your self and add it to your read, or not read yet piles. Here you can also rate the book and leave some notes about it.
